Rules / Info

League Format

  • SUPERFLEX LEAGUE
  • Wed & Sat night bidding / $Zero$ bids allowed / 6 day waivers
  • No divisions, no kickers
  • Playoffs: 6 teams, weeks 15, 16 & 17
  • Playoff FAAB does NOT reset (save some $)
  • Playoffs reseed after round 1
  • **Please do not bid if eliminated from $**

Keeper Rules

  • Must be drafted round 8 or later
  • CANNOT drop player
  • Replaces same round pick next draft and can only be kept that one year
  • You can NOT keep an FA

Draft Notes

  • Snake draft, Superflex, no divisions, no kickers
  • Roster: QB WR WR WR RB RB FLEX S-FLEX D
  • 1 min 30 second picks
  • Sign in ahead of time and make sure you can see the league
  • Add players to your queue in case you run out of time or lose connection
  • Do not draft on an IPAD (keepers aren't shown correctly)
  • NO pickups week 1 - make sure you draft a full team

Beware of Yahoo Glitches

  • IR glitch: If you have someone on IR and they become healthy, you can keep them on IR but you will be locked from picking up players UNLESS you entered your bid prior to their status changing. In the meantime you can change your lineup, you will have to drop a player if you put the IR player in.
  • Defense glitch: If you pick up a D and have an open D slot, your new D won't automatically go in there (careful with overseas games).
  • No pickups week 1 (draft a full team)
